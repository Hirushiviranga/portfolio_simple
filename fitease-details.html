FitEase

Fitness & Wellness App: FITEASE is a mobile application concept designed to empower users in their health journey. It features a streamlined interface for tracking nutrition and structured physical training.

Key modules include:

  • Diet Plan: Personalized meal tracking.
  • Weekly Workout: Goal-based training schedules.
  • Analyze: Progress tracking with data insights.

The design emphasizes motivation and clarity, using an energetic color palette to keep users engaged with their daily fitness goals.
