Elivo

Technical Instrument Shop: Elivo is an e-commerce design concept created for a high-end tech store. It features a massive catalog ranging from mobile phones to advanced gaming tools.

The interface follows a modern, clean aesthetic on a MacBook Pro 16-inch frame. I focused on a full e-commerce user journey—from discovering devices to a simplified cart and integrated bank selection for checkout.
