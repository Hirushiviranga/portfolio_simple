Recipe Book

Wise Way Hotel School, Tangalle: This project involved a complete overhaul of the school's culinary documentation, focusing on translation, design, and typesetting.

Key Contributions:

  • Translation: Accurately translated complex culinary recipes from Sinhala to English while maintaining technical accuracy.
  • Design: Managed professional typesetting, typography, and sectioning to create a clean, hospitality-grade layout.
  • Final Output: Produced a polished, print-ready book that aligned with the institution's branding.
