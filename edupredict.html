EduPredict Pro

Student Exam Performance Predictor: EduPredict is an interactive machine learning platform designed to analyze student academic trends and predict exam success probabilities based on behavioral data.

The application implements a dual-algorithm approach for comparative analysis:

  • Logistic Regression: Optimized with standard scaling to provide statistical probability of student success.
  • Random Forest: Utilizing ensemble learning to identify key success factors like study hours and stress impact.

Beyond simple prediction, the system features a robust analytics suite that visualizes feature importance and data distribution. This allows educators and students to see exactly how attendance and previous scores correlate with final outcomes.
