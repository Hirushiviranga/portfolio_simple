Little Vitals

Baby Management System: Little Vitals is a comprehensive health tracking platform for infants. It integrates sensitive data modules including vaccination history, growth metrics (height/weight), and medical consultations.

The UI features professional graphical dashboards that visualize trends for parent easy tracking. I prioritized a trustworthy, clean, and accessible interface to ensure parents can manage vital medical data with confidence and clarity.
