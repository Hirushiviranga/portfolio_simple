Coffee Heaven

This project is a complete Figma design concept for a modern coffee shop application. The app focuses on browsing menus, placing orders, and reserving tables with a cozy digital experience.

The design uses a warm color palette and simple navigation to reflect a real coffee shop feel, ensuring users can navigate seamlessly from selection to checkout.
